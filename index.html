About

Hi and welcome to my page. My name is Fernando Gaol and I am based in Provo, UT. My story as a developer started when I was 13 and I decided to build a game. I was able to pull it off by watching tutorials, but I had no idea how that code worked. From there on, I tried building more complex games, but my abilities were limited. I failed multiple times so I decided to learn more in depth how to code. However, when I was searching for game development on the web, I also found web development. For some reason, web development caught my attention the most so I started taking some courses on Code Academy such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I started coding for hours every day so that I could get better at what I like to do the most. Over time, I improved a lot and gained more skills, but I got to a point where the courses were no longer helping me learn and I hit a plato. That is why I am currently taking a Full Stack Bootcamp at Thinkful. My goal is make to the best out of it and utilize all the resources that I now have available such as mentors, instructors and technical support. I strongly believe that with this cohort, I will be able to fill in the gap and gain the skills I have not yet been able to acquire. Apart from coding, I like to play soccer, volleyball, and play games on the computer such as League of Legends, Call of Duty, and more. I also like learning new languages and spending time with friends and family, even though I spend most of my days on the computer. Other than that, I am married and I don't have any kids yet, but I do plan to have a couple of them after I develop my skills as a web developer.
